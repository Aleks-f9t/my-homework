Goods in Transit Insurance (cargo)

Under this insurance the owner of goods is insured against financial losses, resulting from the damage or loss of goods in the course of transportation.

BULSTRAD VIENNA INSURANCE GROUP will indemnify the total or partial loss of goods, depending on the insured event and the risks covered. Covered under the policy are also expenses made for salvage of goods and minimization of the loss, as well as the average statement costs.
